Final Metric:

ds² = -A(r) dt²/c² + A(r)^(-1) dr² + r² (dθ² + sin²θ dφ²)

Where:

A(r) = 1 + (c² r²)/(2GM) - (r/r_min) * exp(-r_min / r)

Components:
- c: speed of light
- G: gravitational constant
- M: mass of the object
- r_min: minimum radius (bounce)
- f(r): exponential damping to avoid singularity
